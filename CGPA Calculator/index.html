How GPA and CGPA Are Calculated

Semester GPA:
GPA = Σ (Credits × Grade Points) ÷ Σ Credits
Running CGPA:
CGPA = Σ (Semester GPA × Credits) ÷ Σ Credits
Projected Final CGPA:
Projected CGPA = (Current CGPA × Completed Credits + Σ (Expected Future GPA × Future Semester Credits)) ÷ (Completed Credits + Σ Future Semester Credits)
Calculation Example:

If Sem 1 has 20 credits with 8.50 GPA, and Sem 2 has 22 credits with 9.00 GPA:

CGPA = ((8.50 × 20) + (9.00 × 22)) ÷ (20 + 22)
CGPA = 368 ÷ 42
CGPA = 8.76

“CGPA is calculated using credits, so semesters with more credits have a greater effect on the final CGPA.”
